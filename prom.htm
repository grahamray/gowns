Prom Dresses

Each Prom and Homecoming season we update our inventory of dresses with the latest styles and colors for the best selection. Our dresses run from sizes 0 - 24 and are available in several colors. We special order from the top brands as seen in Seventeen, Your Prom and other Prom magazines. We carry designers such as Sherri Hill, La Femme, Faviana and many more.

We keep track of what gowns we sell to a school to avoid repeats. We will NOT sell a gown twice to your school!

Most dresses pictured are available in other colors. For more info please feel free to call, email or stop by.
